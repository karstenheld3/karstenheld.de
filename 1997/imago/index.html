What is IMAGO?

The "IMAGO" project is a group of young people, who make music and try to use the full quality and possibilities of the today's soundcards.
Unfortunately there is no software available that combines professionality ,easy handling and full soundcard support.
So we decided to develope a "low-cost" software package for professional musicians and programmers who want to use their music in games, presentations or any other programs.
The result of nearly 3 years intensive developement are the IMAGO Orpheus (sample-based music editor) ,the IMAGO Wave (sample editor) and the IMAGO Audio Interface wich will be released soon.
