What is ACM?

We are a small group of musicians dedicating their time to computer based music.
The main idea of IMAGO ACM is, that software companies can ask for any kind of music or soundtrack and we can do it faster, better and cheaper than the most high-end musicians.
Please listen to our demo-musics!

What Music?

    Music or complete soundtracks in following formats:
      Sample (WAV/Mpeg/CD-track)
      MIDI (General MIDI/GS/XG)
      Module (MOD/S3M/XM)

    Sound FX in WAV/AU format
